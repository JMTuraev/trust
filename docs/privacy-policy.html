3. Trust AI va uchinchi tomon (Anthropic)

Ilovadagi Trust AI — moliyaviy hamroh chat. U Anthropic PBC (AQSH) xizmatidagi claude-opus-4-8 modeli yordamida ishlaydi. Bu shuni anglatadiki, so'rovingizga javob berish uchun ma'lumotning bir qismi uchinchi tomon serveriga yuboriladi. Quyida aynan nima yuborilishi ochiq yozilgan.

  • Agregat (umumlashtirilgan) moliyaviy xulosa — oylik daromad/xarajat jami, toifalar bo'yicha summalar va foizlar, trend, qarz summalari va muddatlari, jamg'arma odati.
  • Siz AI'ga yozgan xabar matni va shu suhbatning oxirgi bir necha xabari.

3.2. Nima YUBORILMAYDI

  • Kontragentlarning haqiqiy ismlari yuborilmaydi. Ular serverda, tashqariga chiqishdan OLDIN taxallusga almashtiriladi: «Anvar» → HAMKOR_1. Javob qaytgach, taxallus qurilmangizda yana haqiqiy ismga almashtiriladi. Anthropic haqiqiy ismni ko'rmaydi.
  • Xom yozuvlar (har bir tranzaksiya alohida) yuborilmaydi — faqat agregat.
  • Telefon raqamingiz, parol/token va OTP kodlari — hech qachon.

3.3. Ruxsat (rozilik)

Trust AI'dan birinchi marta foydalanganingizda sizdan aniq rozilik so'raladi va nima yuborilishi ko'rsatiladi. Rozilik bermasangiz — ilovaning qolgan barcha qismi (qarz, xarajat, hisob-kitob) to'liq ishlaydi. AI ixtiyoriy funksiya.

Trust — Privacy Policy (English)

Last updated: 17 July 2026

Trust is a two-sided bookkeeping app. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and who it is shared with.

The app does not use the microphone. Trust does not record audio and does not request microphone permission — all input is text only.

1. Data we collect

DataPurpose
Phone numberSign-up and login (one-time SMS code — OTP)
Name (optional)Your profile name shown to counterparties
Financial / transaction recordsDebts, settlements, expenses and income you enter
Counterparty phone numbersTo identify people you create a link with
Trust AI chat historyMessages you send to the AI and its replies (§3)

2. How we use data

  • Secure access to your account;
  • Maintaining debts and settlements with two-sided confirmation;
  • Parsing the text you type into an expense/income entry;
  • Providing financial analysis and suggestions via Trust AI (§3).

3. Trust AI and a third party (Anthropic)

Trust AI is an in-app financial companion chat powered by the claude-opus-4-8 model from Anthropic PBC (USA). This means part of your data is sent to a third-party server to answer your question. Exactly what is sent is listed below.

3.1. What IS sent

  • An aggregated financial summary — monthly income/expense totals, per-category sums and percentages, trends, debt amounts and ages, saving habits.
  • The text of your message to the AI and the last few messages of that conversation.

3.2. What is NOT sent

  • Real counterparty names are never sent. They are pseudonymized on our server before leaving it: "Anvar" → HAMKOR_1. The real name is restored on your device after the reply comes back. Anthropic never sees the real name.
  • Raw per-transaction rows are not sent — aggregates only.
  • Your phone number, credentials/tokens and OTP codes — never.

3.3. Consent

The first time you use Trust AI you are asked for explicit consent and shown what gets sent. If you decline, the rest of the app (debts, expenses, settlements) works fully. AI is optional.

3.4. Retention and control

  • Chat history is stored on our server (Supabase) so you can see it in the app.
  • Anthropic processes the request to generate a reply. Data sent via the Anthropic API is not used to train models (per Anthropic's commercial terms).
  • If you delete your account, your Trust AI chat history is deleted too.
  • You may request deletion of chat history separately via the email below.

3.5. If an AI reply is wrong or offensive

Every AI reply has a flag button beneath it; flagging sends the reply to us for review. The AI can make mistakes — it is not a licensed financial adviser and does not give investment, tax or legal advice.

Cross-border transfer. The services above (Anthropic, Groq, OpenAI, Supabase, Render) operate servers outside Uzbekistan. The app does not collect biometric or genetic data. The app does not use the microphone and does not record audio.
