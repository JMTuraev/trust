Trust — Privacy Policy (English)

Last updated: 15 July 2026

Trust is a two-sided bookkeeping app. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and who it is shared with.

1. Data we collect

DataPurpose
Phone numberSign-up and login (one-time SMS code — OTP)
Name (optional)Your profile name shown to counterparties
Financial / transaction recordsDebts, settlements, expenses and income you enter
Counterparty phone numbersTo identify people you create a link with
Audio recordings (microphone)Voice entry of expenses — audio is converted to text

2. How we use data

  • Secure access to your account;
  • Maintaining debts and settlements with two-sided confirmation;
  • Converting voice commands to text to create an expense/income entry.

3. Sharing with third parties

We do not sell your data. We use the following services to operate the app:

ServicePurposeData sent
SupabaseDatabase and authenticationAll account data (server)
Groq (Whisper)Speech-to-text (primary)Audio recording
OpenAISpeech-to-text (fallback)Audio recording
devsms.uzSending OTP SMSPhone number
RenderBackend server hostingRequests (HTTPS)

4. Transfer and storage

All data is transmitted between the device and our server over HTTPS (encrypted in transit). Data is stored on Supabase servers. Audio is sent to the services above for transcription and is not permanently stored by us.

5. Your rights

  • Access the data in your account;
  • Request deletion of your account and related data via the email below.

6. Children

The app is intended for users aged 18+ (financial bookkeeping).

7. Changes

This policy may be updated. Significant changes will be announced in the app.
