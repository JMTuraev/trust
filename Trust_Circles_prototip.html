A group who save together and take turns receiving the pot — no bank, no interest, just trust.
1Everyone chips in the same amount each round.
2Each round, one member receives the whole pool.
3Rotation continues until everyone has had a turn.
Every payment is confirmed by both sides — the record becomes proof, not just a claim.
